About Us

Musa is a start up company, newly established and producing innovative products made from thrown away bananas, helping the environment with waste pollution. Musa was created by 11 talented individuals, aiming to help the environment and make use of the banana waste. This company was established on September 2024, lead by President Glaiza Sheryl C. Espina and her chief officers.The company's vision is to become a recognized provider of paper made from banana peels, offering high-quality products to customers, while creating a positive and comfortable environment for employees.The company believes in turning waste into opportunity. Our mission is to take banana waste and transform it into affordable, high-quality, and environmental-friendly paper products that would encourage people to use an environmentalfrienldy option of paper. Making a difference, Unleash Creativity, Sustainbly Sourced,and Aspiring to Excellence are M.U.S.A's core values. As our tagline goes, "your thoughts deserves the best paper".
